# Automotive Market Sales Analysis

## Overview
SQL analysis and Power BI dashboard built on 23,906 automotive sales 
transactions covering 30 brands, 154 models and 7 dealer regions 
across 2022 to 2023.

## Dataset Summary

| Metric | Value |
|---|---|
| Total Transactions | 23,906 |
| Total Brands | 30 |
| Total Models | 154 |
| Total Regions | 7 |
| Total Revenue | $671,525,465 |
| Average Price | $28,090 |
| Period | 2022 to 2023 |

## Key Findings

**Brand Performance**
Chevrolet led all brands with 1,819 units. Top 3 brands — 
Chevrolet, Dodge and Ford — accounted for 21% of total volume.

**Year on Year Growth**
Sales grew from 10,645 units in 2022 to 13,261 units in 2023, 
a 24.6% increase.

**Body Style**
SUVs and Hatchbacks dominated at 26.66% and 25.63% respectively, 
together accounting for over 52% of total volume.

**Transmission Mix**
Automatic transmission captured 52.59% share versus 47.41% manual.

**Regional Performance**
Austin led all seven regions with 4,135 units and $117M revenue.

**Buyer Demographics**
Male buyers accounted for 78.6% of transactions. Female buyers 
had a slightly higher average purchase price of $28,277 versus 
$28,039 for male buyers.
